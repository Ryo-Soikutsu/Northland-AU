## Introduction
The Abyssal Dimension (ID-DIM-001, "Abyss") is a higher-dimension space filled with highly-unstable energy particles, which poses a deadly threat to unprepared explorers and travelers. The Abyssal Dimension serves as the home dimension for Site-0-Sanctuary, as well as a common hub for the Northland Foundation to travel between different universes.
## Geometry
The Abyssal Dimension is an _n_-dimension plane which exists between multiple parallel universes. It assumes that each universe can be modelled as a 3+1 brane, restricted within a higher-dimensional plane known as the "bulk" (or "hyperspace"). The Abyssal Dimension would hence be modelled as a hyperbolic bulk with constant negative curvature. This results in an exponential growth between the distance covered and the volume of space accessible. In simple terms, the bulk can be imagined as a stew, with parallel universes being the ingredients in the soup, such as meat or vegetables, and the abyssal dimension as the soup itself. 

Although both abyssal and known space share the same underlying bulk, they reside in distinct dimensions with no natural overlap to permit travel between the two. To traverse between a universe and the Abyssal Dimension, temporary tunnels (or "bridges") must be created through the curvature of the bulk. This is accomplished using an Abyssal Gate Array ("AGA") module, which works as such

1. Calibrate one end of the bridge to the current position in a universe
2. Calibrate the other end of the bridge to a pre-mapped spatial coordinate in the Abyssal Dimension, accounting for curvature
3. Create a localized distortion in the hyperbolic bulk, and open a bridge mouth on both sides
4. Link the two entrances together to create a tunnel, and hold it long enough for ships to pass through before collapsing

Due to the immense amounts of power required to perform dimensional calculations and stabilize both sides of the bridge, this module can only be mounted on capital-class vessels or larger. Any fleets traversing between abyssal and known space are required to have an AGA-equipped ship (typically a Aegis-class fleet support carrier) flying alongside to ensure a return path home. Due to the unstable nature of these tunnels, idling or taking deviations in the abyssal bridges are forbidden, lest the tunnel collapses and stranding any travelers.
## Physics
The Abyss is entirely filled with abyssal matter, particles which possess a highly unstable and entropy-rich field. When these particles' fields come into contact with any form of matter, a small amount of entropy is applied to it, causing the matter to break down and disintegrate into its base atoms. While weak, the highly condensed nature of the Abyss results in any form of matter, regardless of size or material, to be decomposed into its base atoms within seconds or minutes, then completely converted into more abyssal energy particles soon after. 

Under extreme pressure, such as during the opening of an abyssal bridge, large amounts of abyssal particles can be compressed together, forming abyssal plasma. Abyssal plasma is still highly reactive and energetic, but loses its entropic field, making it significantly safer to be around. Abyssal plasma can be harvested using Abyssal Plasma Extraction Units ("APEUs"), ship-mounted modules which allow for the safe location, extraction and storage of hot abyssal plasma from plasma clouds. The plasma can then be processed using capital-class Mobile All-Purpose Plasma Refinery (MAPPR) modules, or at dedicated installations for advanced abyssal plasma processing into usable fuel, components or weapon-grade material.

Due to instability caused by entropy, the laws of physics and time tend to deviate from that present in the universes. As such, a separate set of physics laws must be abided by when designing abyss-faring ships or space installations. 

## Operations in Abyssal Space
Site-0-Sanctuary is located in abyssal space, at approximately the null coordinates of the dimension. It serves as the primary headquarters and research station of the Northland Foundation, as well as the storage location for Foundation experimental spacecraft, aircraft, technological advancements and WMDs. Members of the Board of Directors also reside aboard the installation. 

Site-0-Sanctuary provides the following services to any Foundation-authorized vessel (list non-exhaustive)
- Docking and ship repairs/resupply
- Medical and cloning bays
- Hangar storage
- General and advanced refining of raw materials
- Manufacturing of sub-capital components, weapons, fuel and ships
- Manufacturing of capital/super-capital components, weapons, fuel and ships (requires authorization from the Board of Directors)
- Campus shielding from abyssal entropy

Scattered across abyssal space are spatial anchors, serving as waypoints for AGA-equipped ships to lock onto a safe, pre-mapped coordinate in abyssal space to open bridges to. The surroundings of these spatial anchors are stabilized in a sphere 25km in radius from the center of the anchor. 

Prospector-class autonomous industrial carriers are deployed to massive plasma clouds to harvest abyssal plasma around abyssal space. These carriers typically return back to Site-0-Sanctuary every few system weeks (typically 4-6 weeks on average) to dump their plasmas, and to perform repairs/restock supplies before being deployed again. 

## Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) and Emergency Protocols
When operating in abyssal space, a set of SOPs must be followed at all time. This is to ensure the safety of vessels and their crew, and to prevent Butterfly effects from triggering at Site-0-Sanctuary
- Do not fire weapons that have not been rated abyssal-safe
- Ensure that your ship's Active Protection System (APS) is active when not within Site-0-Sanctuary's campus shield
- Ensure that your ship is equipped with a Grade-B or higher hyperdrive, and that it has Hyperspace Shielding active at all times
- Ensure that Identification Friend-or-Foe (IFF) is active and configured to broadcast on Channel- ███████
- Once within Site-0-Sanctuary's protective shielding, deactivate your ship's APS. Keep your hyperdrive active while inside, but lower to 30% power

In the event of an emergency, following the respective procedures
- Ship's APS/hyperdrive malfunction while not under protection
	- Activate SOS beacon and inform AWACS Sentinel of the malfunction as soon as possible
	- Attempt to warp back to Site-0-Sanctuary and enter campus shielding
	- If unable to activate warp, divert ship's power to shields and wait for a rescue team
- Site-0-Sanctuary's campus shielding is deactivated
	- Overclock ship's APS and fly as fast as possible away from the installation
	- Head to a spatial anchor and wait for further instructions from AWACS Sentinel
