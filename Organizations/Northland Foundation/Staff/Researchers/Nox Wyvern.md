# Introduction
Nox is a Foundation researcher and combat operator. Due to their unusual circumstances and ties with Executive Director Ryo Soikutsu, data related to their identity and past are unknown or redacted in Foundation databases
# Appearance
Nox is a young adult (approximately 20-21 years old) of average height and build, on par with other researchers residing on the Sanctuary. Nox's gender is unknown, and as such tend to be referred to using non-gendered pronouns and identifier words. They has moderately-long brown hair, and wears a pair of spectacles. Their irides are typically emerald green in color, although they are able to change colors depending on Nox's mood and emotions, such as red during combat scenarios or blue when they is in a relaxed state.

Nox's general attire tends to alternate between Japanese streetwear or academia fashion -- an oversized long sleeved shirt and relaxed-fit pants, or a long-sleeved turtleneck and earth-toned trousers, paired with a wool sweater or coat. They wears a watch on his right wrist, and a thin, silver-colored digi-key ring on their left ring finger that he uses for unlocking doors and computers. During more formal occasions, Nox will choose to wear a 2-piece suit instead, a dark blue blazer with black pants and turtleneck. Their blazer has an emblem of the Foundation on the left breast, and a small pin of a long-tailed tit on their right collar. 

During combat or reconnaissance missions, Nox wears either a modified flight suit designed similarly to the CWU 27/P flight suit for air and space missions, or a light armor set for ground and boarding missions. Their flight suit is green in color, with numerous pockets lining the front and side of the suit. Nox's light armor is dim gray in color, lined with an adaptive photonic matrix which allows the suit to altar its color and texture, camouflaging its wearer into the surroundings. Thermal technology is built in to regulate heat signatures from the wearer to better blend in and hide from thermal imaging. The armor's carbon nanofiber structure provides increased flexibility and mobility to the wearer, allowing them to navigate battlegrounds easily. Several hooks and anchors on his back and belt allows for weapon storage, such as blades or handguns. A three-point sling allows Nox to carry an assorted range of larger weapons and equipment for field operations.
# Behavior and Personality
Nox is highly analytical and observant, making them a valuable member of the Foundation's research teams. They are able to process large volumes of quick-moving information accurately, and provide a detailed report or summary of the data provided. They make full use of these characteristics during his reconnaissance operations, using them to evade hostile observations and detecting traps. They are extremely calm, being able to remain cool-headed and focused even in the most intense of situations, such as during combat operations. 

Nox is primarily non-combative and non-confrontational. They will avoid getting himself into fights or heated arguments with adversaries or his fellow researchers, and try to use diplomatic and/or harmless methods to deescalate incidents. In the event that they are forced to fight, Nox will favor attacks that will temporarily neutralize or disable their opponents, such as using flashbangs or hitting certain parts of a human body to stun adversaries. As a last resort, Nox will use lethal force, aiming for quick and painless kills, such as a single shot to their forehead or through the heart.

Not much is known about Nox's past. Only Ryo Soikutsu, whom has shared a physical body with Nox, has knowledge about their memories. Nox is introverted, and shows signs of social anxiety when interacting with unfamiliar faces and peers.

# Skills and Abilities
Nox is able to learn and remember information at superhuman rates and accuracy, making them ideal for complex and specialized research fields such as abyssal science and FTL research. Their extensive knowledge bank comes in handy during missions too, as they can use knowledge of their surroundings to blend in and complete objectives efficiently.

Nox has below-average physical strength, making them a poor candidate for head-on rushes during boarding action or raid missions. What they lacks in raw strength and power, they makes up for in dexterity, agility and fine motor coordination. Nox possesses exceptional marksmanship, able to hit fast and unpredictable targets over long ranges and in suboptimal weather conditions. In close-quarters combat, Nox is able to take advantage of their agility to exploit enemy weaknesses and vulnerabilities with ease, and dodge attacks to avoid being hit.

As a member of MTF-000 "Crimson Squadron", Nox is frequently deployed on strategic operations in hostile environments. They specialize in espionage and other covert operations, where their lack of physical strength poses less of a drawback to the mission effectiveness. Nox will be deployed to sneak into hostile environments, and either extract with confidential and important data, or clear a path for a much larger offensive force to pass through. Alternatively, they may be deployed on high-value assassination missions, where their long-range marksmanship can come in useful. 

Due to their status as a biological human with no anomalous background, Nox does not possess anomalous or otherwise "magical" powers, such as abyssal energy manipulation

## Addendum: Observer's Veil
Observer's Veil is a support-focused technique, and does not have any physical manifestation or effect unlike other abilities of its class. The technique was developed by Nox during the final assessment of the intermediate abyssal energy manipulation course, and serves as their primary ability for field operations.

When activated, Observer's Veil allows Nox to enter a mirror version of the current world or dimension. The mirror world has identical physical rules and limitations to the original, but may have various effects applied, such as healing and regeneration. At the time of writing, Nox's Observer's Veil is able to provide indirect regeneration through accelerated and controlled cell growth. It cannot heal fatal or critical damage, and cannot repair mechanical or non-organic damage. If enabled, Observer's Veil will create real-time translucent visualizations of entities on the other side of the mirror world. 

In order to enter the mirror world, Nox must ensure that they are within 1 meter of a source of reflection, such as a water puddle or a mirror. In addition, they must be able to see their full body in order to pass through the reflection source. Nox does not have to perform special actions to enter the mirror world, but will often choose to dive through the reflection to enter it quickly. 

It is unknown if Observer's Veil can allow other people to pass through the reflection to the mirror world as well, as all recorded instances of Nox using Observer's Veil has been to transport themselves only to safety. It is assumed that Nox can control what can pass through the reflection, as Nox has been seen entering the dimension with several bullets in their body, only to emerge completely unscathed.
