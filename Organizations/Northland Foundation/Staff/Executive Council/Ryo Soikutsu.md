# Introduction
Ryo Soikutsu is one of the key founders of the Northland Foundation, and the current Executive Director overseeing the Information Technology Division. They are also a prominent researcher, being directly related to many of the Foundation's initial inventions and discoveries like the Abyssal Dimension. Due to their anomalous nature, information related to ███ may randomly  appear as redacted or garbled. 
# Appearance
Ryo Soikutsu typically appears as a young adult with an indeterminate gender, estimated to be around 23 to 25 years old, They possess a slightly athletic build, marginally more developed than that of typical ███-combat personnel. Ryo has moderately-long silver white hair, typically tied up in a side-swept fringe covering their left eye. They typically also wear some flavor of eyewear, such as a pair of smart glasses or a digital compound eye module. Ryo does not have a preferred attire or fashion style, and has been observed wearing outfits of either gender. 

Despite regular deployment in combat and high-risk operations, Ryo seldom utilizes standardized armored exo-systems or dedicated combat armor suites. For combat contexts involving high-intensity flight, they employ a custom-engineered flight suit rated for extreme G-force tolerance (in the hundreds of Gs under controlled inertial dampening conditions). The suit incorporates an integrated biometric and sensor network that continuously monitors physiological status, including physiological status, including cardiovascular load, neural strain, and injury risk indicators.For covert operations, such as espionage, reconnaissance, and targeted elimination missions, Ryo utilizes a tailored formal ensemble (typically a tuxedo or business suit) constructed from reinforced smart-fabric materials. This outfit provides discreet ballistic and cut-█████████ protection while maintaining a low-profile appearance suitable for infiltration environments.
# Background
Ryo Soikutsu was a researcher in a small IPC team, which specializes in the research of Aeonic phenomena and incidents. During a routine experiment, Ryo was caught in the danger zone of the experiment equipment, leading to them █████████ Emanator-level abilities and power for a short period of time. The exact details of this experiment and accident were redacted by ███ ███. Later on, Ryo was able to replicate the conditions of the experiment, and was able to grant Emanator powers to themselves indefinitely. Further developments led to Ryo discovering a way to grant Aeonic powers to a regular Pathstrider or Emanator, and successfully demonstrated a proof-of-concept on them. During the short period of time when Ryo was granted Aeonship, they were able to observe the ████ ████ and the Abyssal Dimension that lay beyond it. This led to Ryo forming the Northland Foundation, with the objective to research the Abyssal Dimension and develop technologies to colonize and thrive in it.

Ryo Soikutsu was present for much of the Foundation's growth and developments, including notable inventions, milestones and conflicts. When the Board of Directors was created, Ryo was nominated as Director of the Information ██████████ division. Ryo was also directly involved or responsible for many key inventions from the Foundation, such as cloning technology, abyssal energy manipulation and abyssal plasma processing.

During an experiment on consciousness transfer between bodies, the experiment went astray and Ryo's consciousness was sent to an undefined location. The Foundation spent several months tracking down meta-signatures across multiple universes, before finally tracking Ryo's consciousness to Nox ██████'s physical body. Nox was transferred into Foundation custody before being brought aboard a research vessel, where Ryo's consciousness was extracted and returned to their original body.
