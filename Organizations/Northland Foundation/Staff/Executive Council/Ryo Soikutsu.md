# Introduction
Ryo Soikutsu is one of the key founders of the Northland Foundation, and the current Executive Director overseeing the Information Technology Division. They are also a very prominent researcher, being directly related to many of the Foundation's initial inventions and discoveries like the Abyssal Dimension.
# Background
Ryo Soikutsu was a researcher in a small IPC team, which specializes in the research of Aeonic phenomena and incidents. During a routine experiment, Ryo was caught in the danger zone of the experiment equipment, leading to them attaining Emanator-level abilities and power for a short period of time. The exact details of this experiment and accident were redacted by the IPC. Later on, Ryo was able to replicate the conditions of the experiment, and was able to grant Emanator powers to themselves indefinitely. Further developments led to Ryo discovering a way to grant Aeonic powers to a regular Pathstrider or Emanator, and successfully demonstrated a proof-of-concept on them. During the short period of time when Ryo was granted Aeonship, they were able to observe the Dark Zone and the Abyssal Dimension that lay beyond it. This led to Ryo forming the Northland Foundation, with the objective to research the Abyssal Dimension and develop technologies to colonize and thrive in it.

Ryo Soikutsu was present for much of the Foundation's growth and developments, including notable inventions, milestones and conflicts. When the first Board of Directors was formed, Ryo was nominated to hold the position of Director over the Information Technology division. Ryo was also directly involved or responsible for some key inventions, such as cloning technology, abyssal energy manipulation and abyssal plasma processing.

During an experiment on consciousness transfer between bodies, the experiment went astray and Ryo's consciousness was sent to an undefined location. The Foundation spent several months tracking down meta-signatures across multiple universes, before finally tracking Ryo's consciousness to Nox Wyvern's physical body. Nox was transferred into Foundation custody before being brought aboard a research vessel, where Ryo's consciousness was extracted and returned to their original body.
